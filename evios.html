overview

This project was a short-term collaboration with a client who wanted a concept and designs for a food delivery app that offered delivery people the option to rent delivery vehicles. The challenge here involved creating two flows - one for the customer and one for the driver/delivery person, who should be able to unlock a vehicle and return it easily.

what i did

As a design consultant, I worked directly with co-founder to create a user journey, distill feedback and data from initial user research, and conceptualize the first version of their NFC digital experience.

research

Since this product was a combination of multiple services, I looked at how existing applications and platforms approached delivery and ridesharing. We realized that we would be building this product for different types of users, so I designed two different experiences - one for delivery drivers and one for customers.

taskflow

From there, I assembled a taskflow that outlined the successful order and delivery path for a customer and the driver.

wireflow

After finalizing the taskflow, I assembled low-fidelity screens that outlined major interactions and a high-level view of the screens.

branding

I pulled from food and alternative energy imagery to design a logo that felt sleek and simple.
