Hello, I'm Melody.

I'm a digital product designer who specializes in designing intuitive solutions for data-driven products. My affinity for design began in 2016, when I conceptualized UI for Star Wars vehicles as an intern at EA. I'm especially passionate about the intersection of arts and design and enjoy working across industries and cross-collaboration. Since then, I've had the unique opportunity to work on some cool projects, from delivering a core feature for a popular mobile game to creating semi-automated solutions for Tesla's supply chain operations.


Currently, you can find me in midtown NYC at Virtual Facility, designing facility automation software.
