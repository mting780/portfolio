overview

The UX team at Visceral consisted of two full-time motion graphics/designers, four engineers, a QA engineer, and a product manager. Throughout my time there, I worked most closely with the two other designers to create motion mock ups, and UI.

fictional UI

These screens were mock-ups for a new spaceship that was hijacked by the game protagonists from Imperial forces. As such, I looked to elements from the Star Wars Empire for inspiration for the interface elements and design.

user feedback indicators

One of the primary features I worked on was the design for enemy awareness meters. These were in-game UI elements that informed players how suspicious or "aware" the AI were of their presence during stealth play modes and are an integral part in any action-adventure game with AI.

Motion mock-ups allowed me to present UI in the context of the game and visualize how a user would encounter it in its various states.
